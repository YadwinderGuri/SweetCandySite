    What Makes Werthers Original Special?

    Much has changed since Werthers Original Hard Candies were first created. But some things have remained exactly the same. Our candies are made with only the finest ingredients, and blended together with the same passion and care our first candy maker, Gustav Nebel, brought to every caramel he crafted. That’s how we make sure every Werther’s Original has the familiar taste you love.

    About Werther's Original

    Werther's Original is a brand of caramel candy owned by the German company August Storck KG. The candy is named after the town of Werther in Westphalia, where the company was founded in 1903.
